#### PyCologne Discussions

##### Details

Seit vielen Jahren organisieren wir eine Python-Diskussionsgruppe, die sich mit allen möglichen Themen rund um Python befasst. Gelegentlich finden auch Vorträge statt, doch die meisten Treffen sind offene Diskussionen. Die Themen werden von den Anwesenden vorgeschlagen, und es herrscht eine ungezwungene Atmosphäre.

Thema dieser Veranstaltung: Dein Thema? Bring Themen und Fragen mit.

Diese Treffen finden auf Deutsch statt, daher solltest du zumindest über Grundkenntnisse verfügen, auch wenn wir spezifische Fragen natürlich auf Englisch beantworten können.

Wir treffen uns normalerweise am ersten Mittwoch jeden Monat am jeweils angegebenen Ort.

For many years we have been organizing a Python discussion group that deals with all kinds of Python related topics. Occasionally we also have talks, but most meetings are open discussions. The topics come from those present and there is an informal atmosphere.

Topic this event: Yours? Bring Topics and Questions.

These meetings are held in German, so you should at least know the basics, although we can certainly answer specific questions in English.

We usually meet on the first Wednesday of every month at the venue specified.

#### PyCologne #

Join us for another evening of Python talks, networking, snacks, and drinks! Connect with fellow Python enthusiasts, learn something new, and maybe even present a lightning talk if you're feeling inspired!

These events are in english!

##### About us

PyCologne warmly welcomes everyone passionate about Python, regardless of expertise level. We formed in spring 2024 after the PyCon DE that year, brought together by a shared enthusiasm for Python and a desire to strengthen the local developer community. Our group is based in the Cologne-Bonn region, and we organize Python-related events in a variety of formats — from meetups and workshops to discussion groups. We encourage participation from the community and host events on a rolling basis at different locations, creating opportunities for networking, collaboration, and learning. At our events we always try to mix beginner-friendly and advanced talks so everyone can find something.

We are constantly looking for new ideas and speakers. Interested? Sign up here.
